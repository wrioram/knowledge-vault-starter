# SOP-003 — New Contact

**Owner:** [your-assistant]
**Trigger:** A new person needs a contact page in the vault

---

## Steps

1. Check `contacts/<Full Name>.md` first
2. Decide whether the person belongs in `contacts/` or `sources/`
3. Create `contacts/<Full Name>.md` using the contact template when appropriate
4. Search existing journal entries for mentions and populate `## Journal mentions`
5. Add an entry to `index.md` under Contacts
6. Append to `log.md`: `## [YYYY-MM-DD] contact | <Full Name>`

## Notes

- Never create a contact page for [vault-owner] themselves.
- Use `sources/` for authors, thinkers, and public figures encountered primarily through their work.
